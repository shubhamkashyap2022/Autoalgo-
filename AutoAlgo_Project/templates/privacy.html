Privacy Policy

We respect your data privacy. No tracking. No ads. No selling data. Open-source & transparent.

Introduction

AutoAlgo Insight ("we," "our," or "us") is an open-source platform for automated algorithm selection and meta-learning. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website, upload datasets, or interact with our tools.

By using our Service, you agree to this policy. We do not collect unnecessary personal data.

Data We Collect

Uploaded Datasets

When you upload a CSV/ARFF file for analysis:

  • File content (features, labels, rows)
  • Meta-features (extracted using pymfe)
  • Temporary storage (deleted after analysis)
Your datasets are never stored permanently or shared.

Technical Logs

We collect minimal server logs:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser & device type
  • Pages visited & timestamps
  • Error logs (for debugging)

No cookies. No analytics. No tracking.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to:

  • Run meta-feature extraction
  • Predict best ML algorithm
  • Improve platform performance
  • Debug errors (anonymized logs)

We do not use your data for training models without consent.

Data Sharing & OpenML

If you choose to fetch datasets from OpenML:

  • We use OpenML API (public datasets)
  • Your IP may be logged by OpenML
  • No personal data is sent

We do not share your uploaded data with anyone.

Data Security

We use:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Temporary in-memory processing
  • Auto-delete after session
  • Regular security audits

No method is 100% secure, but we follow best practices.

Your Rights

  • Access: Ask for logs we have (if any)
  • Delete: All uploaded data is auto-deleted
  • Opt-out: Don’t upload if concerned

Children's Privacy

Our platform is for researchers and students (13+). We do not target children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
